national flower


2021 edition 1
Accordion book, Awagami paper
62 x 76 x 10mm, 27pp

2024 edition 2
Accordion book, Vellum paper
40 x 45 x 10mm, 38pp

self-published


















The images in this book came from many sessions of pretending to walk outside using Google Maps’ street view. It was the pandemic, and I missed being out and about. Whenever I imagine taking a walk outside, there are these patches of pink, magenta, and fuchsia in my imagined peripheral vision.

These are the real national flowers of Singapore.

national flower was first launched with THEBOOKSHOW during the Singapore Art Book Fair 2021, the first edition of the book is now sold out.
