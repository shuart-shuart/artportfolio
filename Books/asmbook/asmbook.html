In the Aisle


2019
Newsprint Zine
25 x 33 x 0.8cm, 60pp +16pp
self-published












In the Aisle gathers titbits of conversations and textures scavenged from Chinese Supermarkets in Edinburgh. These supermarkets function as a third space, playing crucial social roles within the Chinese diasporic community.

Driven by the need to mine for social and emotional familiarity as a way of coping, Shu found herself comfortably nestled within sights and smells reminiscent of home. The most humble and banal conversations gave pulse to these spaces while the scents and scenes the anxieties of being in a foreign land.

In the Aisle was made during the occasion of a 14-day study trip to Edinburgh that was organised by NTU School of Art, Design and Media. The work was selected as part of a student showcase at the Pingyao International Photography Festival 2019, and shown in Bearings at the ADM Gallery.
