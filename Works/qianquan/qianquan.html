迁泉 shifting springs


2023–
Experimental Prints and Video Installation












How do you remember a spring which is already dried up? In its place, we find the imageries and imaginations of the water body on user-generated platforms like Youtube. These amateur and professional videos act as a time capsule where the spring is everlasting and morphing.

泉里的水都干了以后就只剩下“白”–空白的白, 一个视觉想象的游乐场,一个真空的记忆。 YouTube作为一个用户创作平台也是一个畅想的游乐园,也是大众想象的时间囊。

迁泉 shifting springs was presented as part of A Reservoir in Time, curated by Goh Chun Aik as part of Objectif's Curator's Open Call 2023.
