互 deviations


2020
Moving image with Computer-generated audio
2.53min








The sequence unfolds as a looping seascape where waves converge and collide into new forms. The perpetual motion evokes both coastlines and the female form–both unique and resistant of a clear-cut definition. A digitally woven soundtrack of the sea accompanies the image, at once familiar and slightly uncanny, inviting viewers into a space of reflection on perception, culture, and self.

互 deviations was made as part of 临摹 Lapping Emulations.
